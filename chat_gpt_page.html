Who We Are

Our Story

Health in the Margins is a community-led project building spaces for ancestral knowledge, collective care and healing. We work to recenter subjugated knowledge systems and practices in global health.

Our Values

We believe that frontline communities face health crises but their knowledge is often erased. We stand for epistemic justice as health justice, reclaiming the right to know, heal and define health our way.

Our Work

We facilitate programmes, workshops and research that centre frontline communities’ knowledge and support co-creation of new approaches to health and healing.
