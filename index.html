Recentring subjugated knowledge systems and practices in global health action

Our Mission

Striving towards epistemic justice, our mission is to centre the voices, values and healing traditions of frontline communities to advance health equity.

Our Vision

A world where epistemic justice is restored such that frontline communities can use our own knowledge systems and practices to lead global health action and maintain community health and wellbeing.

The Problem

Frontline communities experience the most immediate and severe health impacts of structural violence, environmental degradation, infectious disease, poverty, and inequitable healthcare systems. Our knowledge and healing approaches are excluded from mainstream health practice — this is epistemicide.

Call to Action

Epistemic Disobedience! Recognise and centre the validity, value and role of frontline communities’ diverse knowledge systems, lived experiences and practices in global health action.
